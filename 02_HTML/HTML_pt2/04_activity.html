Activity 1

Create a single page site using a heading, list and link. Use the page to explain an idea, or tell a story about yourself.

Some site ideas to guide you

  1. Personal site - tell us a little about yourself!
  2. Special topic of your choosing - introduce the world to an area of interest with a general overview

Create a single web page for your choice. Begin by making a plan for what you want to include. When you have a plan, share it with the group! Please refer to the HTML Element Glossary to help you choose and use elements!
