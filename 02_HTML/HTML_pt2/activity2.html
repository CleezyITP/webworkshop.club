Create a Multipage Site

Create a multi-page site by creating a new html file, then linking your single page site created in Activity 1 to it (and vice versa). You will need an anchor tag in each html file.

Some secondary page ideas to guide you:

  1. Personal site - after introducing yourself on the main page, provide a link to your history, interests, contact info, etc.
  2. Special topic of your choosing - after giving a general introduction to your toping, branch out to a more specific aspect on a new page. For example, if you introduced the history of Andrew Heiskel on your main page, link to a history of the audio-book project.

Please refer to the HTML Element Glossary and Multipage sites lesson for guidance, or ask the group!
