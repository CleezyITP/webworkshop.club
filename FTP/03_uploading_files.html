How to upload files to your webserver

CNow that you have made a connection, it's time to upload files to your site. Follow the steps below to put the files on your site.

  1. Press Alt + Up arrow. This will open a dialog box
  2. find the folder you made your activity in. choose that folder to upload. This will upload all your files to the site.
  3. you will get 2 notifications that you have an insecure connection. This is atill ok. select connect each time.
  4. when the folder and files have finished uploading, you will get a notification. Your files are now on the webserver!
  5. Press Alt + F4. This will close a status window.
  6. open Internet Explorer and go to the URL we provide you, congratulations, your pages are live!
