FTP

So far, we have been working with webpages that are on our computers, but they have not been online. If a page is only on your computer, you can't share an address to other people because your computer is not a server. A server is a special kind of computer that is always connected to the internet. The server sends pages to browsers when requested. To make your webpages available to the world, you need to store them on a server

To store pages on a server, we something called FTP, which stands for File Transfer Protocol. There are many different FTP programs, we will be using one called Cyberduck.
